FAQs  Please click on the question, the answer will appear.

1. Operation
Q　Can the operator use the birds-eye view from the simulator?
No, the operator can use only information from the sensors installed on the robot.


2. Robot model
Q　Can a team use an original robot model developed by the team? If so, the robot that scores a lot of points but is unrealistic will be participated to JVRC.
The team can use a robot model either provided by the JVRC organizer or developed by the team. Reality of the robot model is screened by the document examination.
Q　When a team install the attachments such as the sensors to the robot model, the location and the numbers are limited?
The location and the numbers of the robot attachments are not limited if they are realistic.


3. Contest
Q　Are the field models available beforehand?
Yes. The models are provided beforehand. The date of the provision is announced separately.
Q　Do the specifications and the decode software of the QR codes are available beforehand?
QThe specifications of the QR codes are provided beforehand. The date of the provision is announced separately. The decode software is not provided.
Q　Which elapse time is used for scoring, real one or simulation one?
The elapse time in simulation is used for scoring.


4. Available software
Q　What software module is provided by the organizer? What module is developed by the team?
The organizer provides a simulation software "Choreonoid" and models of the robots and the tasks. The teams need to develop a control software.


5. Task O1
Q　How many targets locate in the task? Can team know the number of the targets beforehand?
The number of the targets is provided beforehand. The date of the provision is announced separately.
Q　Is the location of the targets reported from the origin?
Yes. The location of the targets should be reported from the origin.


6. Task O2
Q　Does the robot start on the scaffolding?
Yes. The robot starts on the scaffolding.


7. Task R1
Q　What is the interface of the vehicles already available in the field, e.g., a gas pedal, a brake pedal, and a handle?
Yes.


8. Task R2
Q　Are the all combinations of ‘M’, ‘L’, ‘s’, and ‘u’ used for the conditions and specifications of the field?
Any of the combinations of ‘M’, ‘L’, ‘s’, and ‘u’ are used. The combinations are announced separately.
Q　What is “3D information describing the vehicles' qualitative condition”? For example, is a 3D mesh model OK?
Any 3D model that a human (judge) can understand is considered to describe the qualitative condition. A 3D mesh model with reasonable mesh size is OK.


9. Task R4
Q　Are the specifications of the tools, e.g., location of spreader switch, available beforehand?
Yes. The specifications of the tools are provided beforehand. The specifications are announced separately.
